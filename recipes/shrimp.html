Garlic Shrimp

Description

This easy garlic butter shrimp is succulent shrimp tossed in an easy garlic and lemon sauce. The perfect quick dinner or appetizer! If you love garlic, you'll love these lemony, garlicky shrimp which are on the table in less than 10 minutes.

Sometimes the most simple preparations of food can be the most delicious. You don't need to add a whole lot to shrimp to turn them into a culinary delight. These garlic butter shrimp have just a few ingredients and can be on the table in minutes. They're packed with flavor and perfect served as-is or over pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 and a half tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • 6 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons cold butter, cut into 4 equal pieces, divided
  • 1 teaspoon water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a heavy skillet over high heat until it just begins to smoke. Place shrimp in an even layer on the bottom of the pan and cook for 1 minute without stirring.
  2. Season shrimp with seasonings listed; cook and stir until shrimp begin to turn pink, about 1 minute.
  3. Add garlic; cook and stir for 1 minute.
  4. Stir in lemon juice and 1 piece of butter; cook until butter has melted, about 1 minute.
  5. Reduce heat to low and stir in remaining 3 pieces butter. Cook and stir until butter has melted, sauce is thick, and shrimp are pink and opaque, 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Remove shrimp with a slotted spoon and transfer to a bowl; continue to cook butter sauce, adding water, 1 teaspoon at a time, if too thick, about 2 minutes.
  7. Season with salt to taste; serve shrimp topped with the pan sauce. Enjoy!
